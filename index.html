About

The objective of NonProfitID is to be a decentralized digital identity platform for management, mobilization, communication, relationship, and collective agreement in third sector organizations such as unions and non-profit organizations.

The project is developed by the Blockchain-based Tools and Applications research group at the Federal University of São Paulo, in partnership with the Union of Data Processing Workers of the State of São Paulo (Sindpd-SP) and the startup IDook.

The system architecture is built using the Hyperledger Indy, Ursa, and Aries tools.
